Overview

This project demonstrates the use of generative AI tools to create three unique AI avatars. Each avatar represents a different identity or concept and was generated using modern AI platforms for image, voice, and video synthesis. The goal of this project is to explore how artificial intelligence can simulate human appearance, voice, and creative storytelling.

Avatar 1: Game Developer Vision
Description:
 Above is a picture that I imagine would look like a possible me.  Imagine yourself, but a real-life game designer and programmer who makes a living by making video games for players all around the world. This should spark your imagination to try making these types of images yourself too! 

Generative AI Tool Used:
 a2e.ai

Avatar 2: Future Software Engineer AI 
Description:
This avatar,  is a photo-realistic future clone of myself that has been generated by AI. It captures my face and voice and generates a talking clone of me. This shows how AI can master human clone identity for purposes of communication, education and digital media. This was the clone avatar I was required to generate.

Generative AI Tool Used:
HeyGen

Avatar 3: AI Mentor 2050
It‘s Year 2050. You log into this future app and notice a cute AI on the other end who becomes your first teacher an AI that is your avatar. 

Description:
This avatar represent a 2050 AI mentor. The avatar‘s aim is to promote new speculative technology in the 2050s. The avatar talks about how technology will be able to do education,  boost our creativity,  or helping us achieve better standards of life in the future.

Generative AI Tool Used:
HeyGen
